About World of Wrecks

World of Wrecks is a collaborative project dedicated to documenting, mapping, and sharing information about underwater wrecks from all around the world. Our mission is to make maritime history and underwater cultural heritage accessible to everyone through interactive maps and detailed wreck profiles.
